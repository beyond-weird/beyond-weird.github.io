VERSO COL. XIV.

(1) To make ... (2) alum, 1 drachm, (3) pepper, 1 drachm, (4) mhnknwt, dried, 4 drachms, (5) satyrium, 4 drachms.(6) Pound together into a dry medicament; do your business with it (7) like that which you know with any woman.
