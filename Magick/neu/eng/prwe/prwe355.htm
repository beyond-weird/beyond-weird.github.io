THE TINNER OF CHYANNOR

IN Trengothal stood a low hut called the Ram's House. This was said to have been built by the tinners, and called Chyan-nance or House in the Valley. Nor, in Williams's Welsh Dictionary, is earth, This makes Chyan-nor, or the House of Earth.
