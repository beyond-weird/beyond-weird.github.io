FUIGHEAL [216]

FRAGMENT

 

 

p. 216

 

p. 217

MAR a bha,
Mar a tha,
Mar a bhitheas
Gu brath,
A Thrithinn
Nan gras!
Ri traghadh,
’S ri lionadh!
A Thrithinn
Nan gras!
Ri traghadh,
’S ri lionadh!

 

As it was,
As it is,
As it shall be
Evermore,
O Thou Triune
Of grace!
With the ebb,
With the flow,
O Thou Triune
Of grace!
With the ebb,
With the flow.
